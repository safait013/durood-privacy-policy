Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PrimeByte Labs ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in the Durood Sharif Collection mobile application ("the App").

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The Durood Sharif Collection app is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including:

  • Name, email address, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Any other personally identifiable information

2. Data Storage

All data used by the app (such as Tasbih counter progress) is stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to any external server or third party.

3. Internet Permission

The app requests internet permission solely to load Arabic and Bengali fonts (Google Fonts) for proper display of Islamic text. No personal data is transmitted during this process.

4. Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services. We do not share any information with third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
